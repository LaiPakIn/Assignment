Airbnb, Inc is an American vacation rental online marketplace company based in San Francisco. Instead of holding any of the real estate listings, the company operates as a broker, receiving commissions from each booking. Airbnb offers accommodations for temporary residence, typically for homestays or tourism experiences. Since 2008, guests and hosts have utilized Airbnb to explore more travelling possibilities and present a more personalized way of experiencing the world. This dataset is downloaded from Kaggle.com, which holds free public data published by Inside Airbnb. It contains approximately 50 thousand Airbnb listings in NYC as of August 2019. The purpose of this project is to construct a model which can effectively predict the price of NYC Airbnb rentals based on factors such as neighbourhood group and room types.

It is defined that "price" is the Y variable and the rest are the X variables. I started by cleaning the data and removed unnecessary X variables from the dataset for simplification in the further analysis. I have analyzed each variable and determine the boundary that allows us to eliminate extreme values that may cause an "outlier effect" on the regression analysis later on. I have also created dummy variables by combining different groups with similar characteristics within a particular X variable.
